JOYBLIND at BarCraft - MLG Orlando
By: Lucas Hudson - Wed, 26 Oct 2011 19:03:09

This past weekend, the JOYBLIND crew got together with other eSports fanatics around Ottawa to take part in a movement sweeping North America and the world: BARCRAFT. For those of you who do not know what BarCrafting is, here is a quick overview. BarCraft is very similar to what typically goes on in a sports bar or other pubby type place when football or baseball is on, except a much cooler game is on the television.

People gather around to watch StarCraft II or League of Legends (and any other game for that matter) being played by the best in the world and do it while surrounded by people enthusiastic about games. Not only are you surrounded by awesome people but your in a bar! You can order great food, a couple drinks and sit back, relax and cheer at the top of your lungs when the final GG of the tournament is typed into chat. To say the least, BarCraft is an experience that one cannot miss out on.
